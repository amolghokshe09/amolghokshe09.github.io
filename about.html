IoT Weather & Climate Monitor

      We will be solving issues regarding Air Quality using the, IoT(Internet of Things), Big Data Analytics technology. Using the combination of these technologies we hope that we will be able to understand relations between Air quality parameters and weather conditions, as these two are connected to each other and studying and analysing the pattern between them we will be able to solve many problems regarding Air Quality and Weather. If we are able to solve Air Quality issues, we can also solve many problems that are caused by weather and air contamination like drought, famines, hurricanes, etc.
       All the data received from Drones and Air purifiers to the servers will be analysed along with the weather conditions and factors like Atmospheric Temperature, etc using Big Data. This will help researchers from all over the world to understand the connections between air pollution and weather conditions and help to tackle natural calamities like Hurricanes and Famines.
